Thread Revival

Thrift. Style. Rebirth.

Thread Revival is a fictional NYC-based fashion pop-up that celebrates secondhand style, sustainability, and self-expression. Inspired by events like Thrift2Death, it brings together local thrift vendors, upcycled fashion designers, and vintage curators for one bold, day-long experience. This event is especially for young creatives, sustainable fashion advocates, and anyone ready to express themselves through bold, affordable style.

Event Highlights

  • 30+ vendors selling thrift, vintage, and Y2K fashion
  • Live DJ sets and curated zones
  • $25 fill-a-bag sales for early birds
  • Applications open for new vendors
